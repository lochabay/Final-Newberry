The Cards of Euchre

Our cards are handmade and illustrated with the characters found in the lumbercamp of Newberry. You'll find hunters, bears, lumberjacks, raccoon and many more forest critters. There's even cabins and outhouses...(do you know what an outhouse is?)!


It's a forest of cards filled with great characters! Meet the players of Euchre!

Aces

The Aces of Newberry are represented by the rustic cabins, which are the foundation of the history of Newberry. With the exception of the trump suit Jacks, the Aces are the highest card.

Kings

The hunters of Newberry are kings in these parts. Well, most likely you'll find them asleep in their deer blinds, but you never know when a king will come in handy!

Queens

The queen of the woods is the Mama bear. You'll find out why if you ever cross her path and she has her cubs with her. Don't worry...you'll never have to outrun a bear if you bring friends that run slower than you.
